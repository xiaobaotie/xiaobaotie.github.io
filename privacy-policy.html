1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Color Pop 8 ("the App") by ListeningStudio handles information. The App is a casual puzzle game. It does not require account sign-in and does not ask users to submit personal information within the App.

2. What information is collected

  • No personal data is collected by the App itself. The App does not request name, email, contacts, photos, precise location, or similar personal data.
  • Advertising and device identifiers. The App integrates Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). Google and its advertising partners may access the Android Advertising ID (AD_ID) and other device signals to show, measure, and improve ads. See Google’s privacy policy linked below.

3. How information is used

  • To display banner and other Google ads, including frequency capping, fraud detection, and performance measurement.
  • The developer does not use collected data to build user profiles beyond advertising served by Google.

4. Data sharing

The App shares data only with Google/AdMob for advertising purposes. No other third parties are integrated.

5. Your choices

  • You can reset or delete the Android Advertising ID in Settings → Google → Ads (path may vary by device/Android version).
  • You can opt out of personalized ads from Google in device settings and Google account ad settings.

6. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under the applicable age of digital consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Security

We rely on Google’s industry-standard security to handle advertising-related data transmitted to Google Mobile Ads.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or requests about this Policy, contact 911164435@qq.com.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
